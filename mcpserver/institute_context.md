Building Access and Security
Card Access
Access to the Institute building is granted via the use of an access card through the main entrance. You will receive your card on your first working day.
Ensure you always carry your card. With your card, you have access to spaces that relate to your position and needs. The building layout by floor and main rooms can be found in Appendix 1.
Employees may access the GATE building Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM. All employees are required to leave the building by 8:00 PM. If you must stay past 8:00 PM, please notify both the Deputy Director of Administration and Finance and your supervisor. They will provide further instructions on how to leave the building once you’re done with your work.

Front Desk Security
Our front desk is attended by security personnel to ensure the safety and security of everyone in the building.

Parking Access
For employees’ convenience, parking spaces are provided on the GATE Institute premises. To gain access to these parking areas, you must apply for a pass allowing entry to the Lozenets campus of Sofia University "St. Kliment Ohridski". The application must be filled in using a form which can be obtained from the Office Manager.

Working Hours
GATE Institute’s standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM Monday to Friday with a 30-minute lunch break. Opening hours for Front desk security are 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM every day except weekends and national holidays. Hygienists work Monday to Friday from 7:00 AM to 3:30 PM.
GATE Institute observes the national holidays of Bulgaria, considering them as non-working days. As part of Sofia University “St. Kliment Ohridski,” GATE Institute also complies with the orders of the Rector of the University to merge days off around holidays such as Easter and Christmas.

Flexible Working Hours Policy
GATE Institute's flexible working policy enables employees to achieve an optimal work-life balance. Employees can work between 7:00 AM and 8:00 PM, allowing everyone to adapt their schedule, promoting productivity and combining work with personal commitments. GATE Institute believes in creating a dynamic work environment that values individual needs while maintaining employee dedication to excellence.

Breaks and Lunch
All employees have a designated time for physiological breaks and lunch break. The breaks are as follows:
Physiological breaks:

Two breaks of 15 minutes each for employees on standard working hours. One is taken before lunch and the other after the lunch break;
Two breaks of 30 minutes each for security staff at reception. One is taken before lunch and the other after the lunch break;
Lunch breaks:

A 30 minute lunch break for employees on standard working hours, which is not fixed and depends on workload and is taken between 12 PM and 3 PM;
60 minute lunch break for Reception staff (porters), which is not fixed and depends on workload and is taken in the range 12 PM and 3 PM


Conference and Meeting Rooms
Scheduling Procedures
For use of conference or meeting rooms please refer to Appendix 2.
Room Etiquette
Respect the scheduled time, keep noise to a minimum, and ensure the room is left in the same condition it was found. Report any malfunctions immediately to the Facility Manager.

Kitchen and Kitchenettes
Kitchen Guidelines
The main kitchen is located on floor 0, there are kitchenettes on floor 2 and floor 3, all of which are shared spaces. Please keep these spaces clean and tidy. Report any problems to the building steward.

Dishwashing
After use, please either wash used dishes or place them in the dishwasher. When the machine is full, please run it.

Dining Rules
Eating in work areas is absolutely prohibited to promote a clean and healthy work space. Eat in designated areas. It is recommended to eat lunch in the kitchen on floor 0 to encourage socializing and easier maintenance of the space given the floor covering.

Using Printing Machines
Printers are available on the second and third floors for use by any employee. Additionally, there are printers in the labs, as well as ones designed specifically for printing event materials. Access to a specific device requires prior approval from a direct supervisor and is provided by the IT department.

Phone Book
A telephone book with all internal telephone numbers of the GATE Institute staff and their office numbers can be found in Annex 3.
